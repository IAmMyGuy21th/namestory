Jayden's Name Story

  1. My mother selected the name Jayden for me because it was quite popular at the time.
  2. Jayden is a gender-neutral name with Hebrew origins. The name is a modern invention and is considered a variation of the biblical name Jadon. Jadon was a man who helped restore the walls of Jerusalem in the Old Testament. (https://www.nameoftheyear.com/jayden-name-meaning/)
  3. My mother personally chose and gave me the name Jayden.
  4. The specific spelling of my name was also chosen by my mother.
  5. I decided on the name Jayden because it has significance to a family member.
  6. Personally, Jayden signifies being thankful to me.
  7. I have a vivid memory of when my teacher called out "Jayden" and two classmates responded with "here".
  8. I'm not particularly fond of the name as it is quite common.
